EduReply is a web‑based application designed to help teachers automatically generate responses to student inquiries.

Teachers can configure a list of authorized student email addresses in the Authorized Students section and define Q&A keywords in the Knowledge Base. When an email is received from an authorized student, the system uses AI to generate a draft reply. Teachers may review, edit, and approve the draft before it is sent back to the student.
In Email Settings, you must provide the required SMTP (outgoing mail) and IMAP (incoming mail) configuration details.

Important Notice:
Some university email systems may block outgoing messages due to strict firewall and security policies. Many institutions enforce DMARC/SPF rules that reject emails appearing to come from @gmail.com when they are sent through third‑party services such as SendGrid. This may prevent EduReply from successfully delivering messages to university email accounts.
